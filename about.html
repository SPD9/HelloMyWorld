About Us

Bubble under is a group of diving enthusiastics based in
the south-west UK who meet up for diving trips in the summer
months when the weather is good and the bacon rolls are flowing.
We arrange weekends away as small groups to cut the costs of
accommodation and travel and to ensure that everyone gets a
trustworthy dive buddy

Although we are based in the south-west, we don't stay on our
own turf.
past diving weekends away have included trips up to
Scapa Flow in Scotland and to Malta's numerous wreck sites
 when we are not diving, we often meet up in a local pub to talk
about our recent adventures
